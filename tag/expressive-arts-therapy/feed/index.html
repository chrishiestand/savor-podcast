Donna shared the following list of resources for our readers:

Start (or keep) Creating for a Bigger Life and Happier Body Image

How Expressive Arts Works:

Art Heals, Art as Medicine, Trust the Process, by Shaun McNiff

Art is a Way of Knowing, by Pat Allen

These Books Make You Want To Make:

Mighty Ugly by Kim Werker

Wreck This Journal by Keri Smith

Fearless Creating by Eric Maisel

Marry Your Muse by Jan Phillips

The Artist’s Way, by Julia Cameron

Steal Like An Artist & Show Your Work, by Austin Kleon (great newsletter & Tumblr too)

Messy Artist Books to Inspire You:

Spilling Open by Sabrina Ward Harrison

The Journey is the Destination by Dan Eldon

Land Art:

Andy Goldsworthy: A Collaboration with Nature by Andy Goldsworthy

Movies:

Four-Eyed Monsters

It’s Kind of a Funny Story

Check Out Donna’s Snail Mail Art Website At:

LongLiveSnailMail.com

Donna will be offering Create Your Way To A Better Body Image workshops in San Diego this coming May and if there is enough interest, in Portland as well.
